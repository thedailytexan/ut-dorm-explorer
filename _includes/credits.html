Methodology

Data for ratings and reviews were gathered from respondents to a survey distributed by Daily Texan staff. Student reviews were reviewed and selected by Daily Texan staff.

Ratings were averaged for each of the four categories, and then aggregated again for the composite rating. Sample sizes may differ amongst different residence halls.

Price data were gathered from the official UT residence hall descriptions, and the lowest price was selected for each residence hall.
